Pizza

Although I did say there would be more pizza here, Unfortunately, The Space Racoons have stolen the rest of the pizza contents, recipes and chefs.

Fortunately there is still one pizza recipe here and it is my favourite, The Hawaiian.

Requirements

Parts Ingredients Amount
Pizza Base All-purpose flour 400 grams
Sugar 2 tbsp
Salt 1 tsp
Instant Yeast 3/4 tsp
Warm Water 100ml
Olive Oil 2 tbsp
Toppings Tomato Sauce 250ml
Grated Cheese Any
Oregano 1 tsp
Ham Pieces Any
Pineapple Pieces Any

Steps, with video below

  1. Dump pizza base ingredients into a mixing bowl, excluding the oil and warm water.
  2. Mix ingredients with whisk and then add the warm water with a tbsp of olive oil. The other tbsp is for greasing later.
  3. Prepare flat surface with some flour and place dough mixture on it. Remember to coat hands in some flour to prevent sticking. Knead for at least 5 minutes until it's soft and elastic.
  4. Cover a large bowel with the remaining oil. Place kneaded dough there and also cover it in oil. Cover the bowl with a towel and leave until dough has seemingly doubled in size through sheer will and determination. This may take more than 2 hours.
  5. Once risen, punch the centre of it to discourage mutiny and remove it from the bowl. Place the dough on a pizza pan and stretch it with hands or use a rolling pin to expedite the dominating process.
  6. To remove air bubbles, perforate the flattened dough using a fork several times. This will additionally further your authority over the dough as it realises how powerless it currently is.
  7. Once the dough has become fully submissive, cover it with the tomato sauce using a spoon and the spoon's back to efficiently spread it across the base.
  8. Apply the cheese, oregano, ham and pineapple. Additional toppings may be added if you wish. Place in oven at 200℃ for 25 minutes or until most of the outer crust have browned.

Caution: Keep watch over pizza to prevent burning at all times.
